Our Story

Marico Coffee and Food is a premium cafe in the heart of Kathmandu. We serve freshly baked croissants, artisan cakes, and specialty coffee crafted with care. Every detail — from our food to our atmosphere — is designed to make your visit memorable.

Our commitment is simple: use only the finest ingredients to create baked goods and coffee that not only taste incredible but make you feel at home the moment you walk through our doors.
