Our Story

Welcome to Marico Bakes And Bites, where passion meets flavor in the heart of Mandikhatar, Kathmandu. Founded with a love for authentic baking, we've been serving our community with natural, healthy ingredients since day one.

Our commitment is simple: use only the finest natural ingredients to create cakes, bakery items, pizzas, and coffee that not only taste incredible but also make you feel good about what you're eating.
